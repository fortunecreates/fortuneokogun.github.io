What I Have Built

Process Design · Automation · Onboarding

Community Member Onboarding Automation

The problem: a growing global community was onboarding new members through a public WhatsApp link with no system to collect information, no structured process, and admins manually chasing people for basic details.

Designed and built a fully automated end-to-end member onboarding system from scratch — replacing a manual, inconsistent process with a structured, professional experience from registration to group access.

  • Built a structured intake form collecting member details and photo before group access is granted
  • Wrote an Apps Script automation that fires a personalised welcome email instantly on form submission
  • Designed a branded landing page with group values, meeting schedule, platform links, and community website
  • System naturally filters for committed members — only those who complete the form receive group access
  • Zero manual admin required from the organisation after setup
